Peanut Butter Monster

This quick and easy smoothie is packed full of protein to give you a simple post-workout recovery shake.

Difficulty Medium
Prep 10 Mins
Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tsp Peanut Butter
  • 1 Scoop Vanilla Protein Powder

    How To Make

  • Step 1

    Chop bananas into slices

  • Step 2

    Put the ingredients in a blender
